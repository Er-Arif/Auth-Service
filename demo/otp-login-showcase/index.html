OTP Login Showcase

This page shows exactly how another product can use your auth service: collect email, send OTP, verify it, create a session, and fetch the logged-in identity.

Flow

  1. Enter email
  2. Call /otp/send
  3. Enter the OTP from email
  4. Call /otp/verify
  5. Use the returned tokens

Local demo note: this page includes app credentials for learning and testing only. In a real product, app secrets should stay on your backend.
